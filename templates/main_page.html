About Us

Welcome to Pets-R-Us! We’re excited to share our new, streamlined inventory system, designed to enhance the efficiency and professionalism of managing inventory for our store. This system was developed by a talented team of three developers—Claire, Holly, and Therese—each of whom brought their unique skills and dedication to the project.

From personal experience with insufficient software and current market offerings for pet digital storefronts, we wanted to improve inventory tracking efficiency. This will provide customers with a less frustrating and more enjoyable experience when shopping for our fur babies. This system creates a fresh take on typical inventory management systems that is more user-oriented and secure when updating inventory levels.

Our Mission

Our goal at Pets-R-Us has always been to make pet ownership easier and more enjoyable. With the new inventory system, we’re now able to provide a more reliable, organized approach to inventory management, allowing our team to focus on what matters most—serving you and your pets. We’re grateful to Claire, Holly, and Therese for their hard work and dedication in making this system a reality!
